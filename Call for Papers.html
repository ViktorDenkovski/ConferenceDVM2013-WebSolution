CALL FOR PAPERS

 

The Organizing Committee welcomes submission of original contributions for oral and poster presentations at the DAYS OF VETERINARY MEDICINE 2013.

The Organizing Committee accepts two types of papers:

  • abstracts that will be published as an in the Proceedings Book.
  • full papers that will be published in the International Journal "Macedonian Veterinary Review"

All papers will be reviewed by International Scientific Committee of the Conference and International Editorial Board of the Journal.

The abstracts will appear exactly as typed, therefore please carefully follow the instructions below:

  • The abstracts should be submitted in ENGLISH only.
  • The title must be typed in capital letters.
  • Authors are urged to include their full surnames, complete with first and middle names.
  • The full name of institutions and subsidiary departments should be given, together with a useful address including postal code.
  • If several authors and institutions are listed on a paper, it should be clearly indicated with which department and institution each author is affiliated.
  • The affiliation address in each case should be indicated by superscript.
  • The name of the speaker-author must be followed by an asterisk (*).
  • The abstracts must be laid out in terms of:
    Introduction-Materials and Methods-Results-Conclusions.
  • Use "Times New Roman" typeface points 11.
  • The length of abstract should be 400 words and with no more than 5 key words.
  • The reception of the abstracts will be confirmed by email.

 

The full papers must be writen according the Journal propositions.
Please download the full guide for Instruction to Authors or example of full paper published in the International Journal "Macedonian Veterinary Review".

 

PAPERS SUBMISSION

Please submit your paper (one version in Word Document Format and one version in PDF) in the form of an e-mail attachment to:

dvm2013@fvm.ukim.edu.mk

Only papers accompanied by the payment of the registration fee will be considered for reviewing, acceptance and inclusion in the Final Program.

The paper submition via fax is not acceptable.

POSTER PRESENTATION

  • Poster size: 70 cm wide and 100 cm high.
  • Lettering should be in dark, contrasting colors.
  • Do not use combinations of red and green as these are difficult for people with common types of color blindness to read.
  • Avoid using large tables of figures - try to use graphs instead.
  • Include photographs and illustrations wherever possible.
  • Keep the text to a minimum; avoid detailed description of well-known methods.
  • If possible, prepare and make available some A4-size paper copies of the poster for interested participants.
  • NO landscape posters will be displayed!

AUDIOVISUAL FASCILITIES

  • For the Conference, video beam projections will be available in the conference room. Presentations should be prepared in "Power Point" (*.ppt file).
  • For special audiovisual facilities, please send in advance a special request to the Organizing Committee, not later than September, 1st 2013.
