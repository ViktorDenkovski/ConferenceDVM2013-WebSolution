Dear Colleagues and Friends,

 

On behalf of Organizing Committee, we are pleased to invite you, for active participation on DAYS OF VETERINARY MEDICINE-2013, which will be held from 6-8 September in Struga, Macedonia.

The organizer is the Faculty for Veterinary Medicine at the University of Ss. Cyril and Methodius in Skopje.

The program offers unique opportunity for plenary lectures, scientific presentations and discussions about animal health, food safety, public health, animal welfare and reproduction.

We believe that this meeting is an excellent opportunity for renewal of old and making new contacts between scientists, veterinary practitioners, farm veterinarians and official veterinarians. Veterinary science has undergone tremendous development in all fields of research and gained ever-increasing importance in the management of many diseases.

Faculty of Veterinary Medicine in Skopje continuously plays a fundamental role in these processes.These activities have generated significant results in various areas of the veterinary science and contributed greatly to the incorporation of science into every day veterinary practice.

The venue will be in the town of Struga which is indeed the cultural history of the Republic of Macedonia in miniature. The town of Struga is ready to welcome all participants offering unique experience through a blend of beautiful lake, museums, old churches and above all traditional Macedonian food.
We look forward to seeing you in Struga for this unique and stimulating event!

The Organizing Committee!
